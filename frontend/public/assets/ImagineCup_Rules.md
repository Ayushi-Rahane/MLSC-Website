# Imagine Cup Official Rules

## 1. Overview
Imagine Cup is Microsoft’s springboard for student founders who seek to build commercially viable solutions using Microsoft’s cloud and AI platforms.
To compete, a submission must use at least two Microsoft AI services.
Submissions must include:
• Completed responses to all questions in the application form, detailing the team’s experience in building technical solutions.
• A pitch deck that includes a demo video of the team’s minimum viable product (MVP).
• A video recording of the team’s pitch.
These materials must collectively address the judging criteria outlined in this document.

## 4. Competition schedule
This Competition starts at 00:00 Coordinated Universal Time (“UTC”) on September 30, 2025 and ends in May 2026. It consists of up to three (3) rounds with two (2) paths for entrants: one path for the Launch teams and one path for the Scale teams.

**Round Dates:**
- **MVP Round:** September 30, 2025 – January 9, 2026
- **Semifinals Round:** February 10, 2026 – April 7, 2026
- **World Championship Round:** April 2026 – May 2026

## 5. Eligibility
You are eligible to compete if you meet the following requirements:
• You are at least 18 years of age as of September 30, 2025.
• All team members must be enrolled students at an accredited educational institution between September 30, 2025 and May 31, 2026.
• You are NOT a resident of prohibited countries (Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Sudan, Syria, Russia, Crimea).

## 6. How to enter
• Register on the Imagine Cup website and create your team.
• Choose either Launch or Scale path.
• Submit your entry via the website.

## 7. Submission requirements
• Product must use at least two (2) Microsoft AI services.
• All materials must be in English.

## 8. Competition details
Encouraged Categories:
• Cybersecurity
• Education
• Finance
• Health and Life Sciences
• Retail and Consumer Goods

### 8.1 MVP Round
Build a functional MVP demonstrating customer validation and market fit.
Submission materials by Jan 9, 2026:
(1) Pitch Deck (max 15 slides).
(2) Recorded Videos: 3-min pitch, 2-min demo.

### 8.3 Semifinals Round
5-week Training Program + Technical Review + Live Pitch and Demo.

### 8.5 World Championship Round
Top 3 Scale teams pitch live to expert judges.

## 9. Judging Criteria – MVP Round
- **Founder insight, market viability, and inclusive design (30%)**: Unique problem definition, market research, diversity consideration.
- **Founder-led validation (30%)**: User engagement, feedback usage.
- **Use of Microsoft Technology (40%)**: Integration of AI services, technical robustness.

## 10. Prizes
- **Semifinalists:** Azure credits ($25k), Replit credits ($1k).
- **Launch Path Winner:** $50k cash + prizes.
- **Scale Path Finalists:** Additional Replit credits.
- **World Champion:** $100k cash + mentorship + partnership opportunities.
